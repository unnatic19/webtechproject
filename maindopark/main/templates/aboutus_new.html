About Us

We , at Maindo park attempt to make our customer's life easier by finding them parking spaces at their desired location. Forget about toeing of your cars, parking in no-parking zones and altercations with the traffic police. All you have to do is register into our website and you are all set! All we wish for is your well-being and attempt to smoothen your parking experience. Wishing you an amazing day!

We know our routes well, so let us guide you to a more comfortable way of life. For the safety and the benefit of the user, we at SPOTVIK have made a dedicated GPS tracking device that you can buy from our website and attach it to your vehicle. This will let you easily track down your vehicle using our mobile app. So now when you visit a Mall no more remembering the pillar number near which you have parked your vehicle and let us do the finding for you! Find the best suggestions of the parking spots near you. The app gives you the privilege of choosing a parking space, from the multiple parking spaces we offer to you near your desired location. We even show the recommended parking space which we feel is best suited for your needs. The recommended location is chosen using the factors like availability of the space and the distance between the parking space and your destination. Want to know your parking spots before even planning a visit? We have got that figured out too! You can even manually type in the area in our search, we can provide you with parking spots. Voila! Want to check where you parked last, well , worry not because we have that figured out too. We have a history option, where you can see all the spots you have parked before . We provide you with details like date, time and the duration for which you have parked.
